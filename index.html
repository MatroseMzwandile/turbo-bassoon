2. Discovery Home Care Disclaimer

Home Care is a service Discovery offers as an alternative to receiving healthcare services in a hospital. This means that you can receive the healthcare services that you would normally receive from medical professionals while you are in hospital, in the comfort of your home. Home Care also provides you with healthcare services in a controlled, safe, sterile, and calm environment to allow a faster recovery. The benefits of Discovery Home Care are noticeable when you follow the treatment protocols, advice, and instructions that the medical professional gives.
